The Red Hat solution

Increasingly, organizations are turning to open standards-based technologies from Red Hat to create an infrastructure that allows the IT organization to go beyond keeping the lights on and, instead, staying innovative. RedHat provides an ideal platform for standardization by delivering proven, enterprise-supported technologies that deliver extraordinary efficiency and value.

An SOE based on Red Hat® technologies simplifies your IT infrastructure so you can operate more efficiently and securely for less. How much less? According to IDC, on average, standardizing on Red Hat infrastructure solutions:

  • Delivers a return on investment of 418%.
  • Pays for itself in 7.1 months.
  • Improves business agility by accelerating the time to market for Linux® servers by 77%, which speeds up the launch of new business applications by 13%.
  • Reduces annual server infrastructure costs by US$3,566 per 100 users.
  • Increases IT staff productivity and lowers labor costs by US$3,318 per 100 users per year.
  • Increases revenue by US$4.2 million by ensuring a more reliable and faster platform.

Red Hat offers a comprehensive infrastructure portfolio, enabling you to realize the benefits of information technology without boundaries. With Red Hat, you can increase datacenter efficiency and uptime to help stretch your budget to address real opportunities for technology-enabled business innovation.

Red Hat Enterprise Linux

Red Hat Enterprise Linux is the world’s leading enterprise Linux platform, relied on by 90% of Fortune 500 companies. This gives you the tools you need to boost efficiency, improve security, speed delivery of new applications, and ultimately prepare your datacenter for an open hybrid cloud IT architecture.

Red Hat Satellite

Do-it-yourself systems management limits efficiency and responsiveness. A fully optimized and automated system delivers a much more manageable and reliable environment that scales with ease and helps you do more with less. Red Hat Satellite provides efficient deployment and management of Red Hat Enterprise Linux standard operating environments, including everything from provisioning to patch, configuration, and subscription management.
