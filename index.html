Introduction

As a senior IT leader, what if you could make 1 strategic change that would save your department time and money? What if it could also significantly improve your ability to be more responsive to the needs of the business? The answer seems obvious.

Many industry-leading organizations are making a fundamental change right now to their IT infrastructures. These forward-looking organizations are standardizing their operating environments and removing layers of complexity to:

  • Lower operational costs.
  • Improve security and compliance.
  • Deliver applications faster.
  • Lay the groundwork for innovation.

Learn how your organization can achieve these benefits and more by moving to a standard operating environment.

Gartner: Standardize as much as possible

“Administration costs (not hardware or software costs) are often the biggest contributor to escalating IT operations costs.”

-Gartner
